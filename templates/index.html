Welcome to HaVoC-EcoWATT!

HaVoC-EcoWATT helps homes and offices optimize energy use with AI, using simple user data instead of IoT meters. It predicts peak hours, suggests cost-saving schedules, and shows usage trends—empowering users to save money and reduce their carbon footprint through smart software insights.

About Our Platform

HaVoC-EcoWATT is a forward-thinking platform designed to revolutionize energy management for homes and offices. By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, our solution analyzes user-input data—such as appliance schedules, electricity bills, and local weather information—to deliver personalized recommendations for energy savings. The system identifies peak usage periods, suggests optimal times for running devices, and provides clear visualizations to help users understand their consumption patterns. With HaVoC-EcoWATT, users can make informed decisions to lower costs, reduce environmental impact, and contribute to a more sustainable future—all without the need for complex hardware installations.
