Our Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with intelligent energy management solutions that reduce costs, minimize environmental impact, and promote sustainable living through cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology.

Our Story

HaVoC-EcoWATT was born from the vision of creating accessible energy optimization solutions that don't require expensive hardware installations. Our team recognized that while IoT devices are powerful, not everyone can afford or install complex monitoring systems.

We developed an AI-driven platform that leverages simple user inputs to deliver sophisticated energy insights. By analyzing appliance usage patterns, electricity bills, and weather data, our system provides personalized recommendations that help users save money and reduce their carbon footprint.
